About

Our gardens can bring us incredible joy, happiness, and fulfillment when done right; not to mention all the fresh, delicious, and nutrient dense produce. I'm here to make your garden, homestead, or plot dreams come to fruition. I focus on sustainablility, suitable climate planting, companion planting, and harvest and/or beauty optimization. Whatever your dream, let's make it a reality. 

Services

Design / Layout

I will help you design the perfect garden, whatever you imagine: an eden of flowers, a stunning wild-like homestead, a produce production-haven... We can even plan your garden for the whole year.

Plant List

You will receive a plant list, with all the plants you will need to make your dream a reality. The plants will be climate-specific and perfect for your region.

Guidelines for Optimal Growth

I will create a guideline manual for your specfic plants, with information regarding proper transplanting, a watering schedule, what each plant needs to grow optimally, and maintenance tips.

Consulting Calls

We will schedule a time anytime you have questions, concerns, or just need some advice on your garden dream.
